About UPL

The Uttarakhand Premier League is the state’s premier T20 tournament conducted by the Cricket Association of Uttarakhand. With the inaugural season slated to be held in June 2024, involving 6 men’s and 3 women’s’ teams, expect plenty of fast-paced T20 action. To be played at the Rajiv Gandhi International Cricket Stadium, Dehradun, the league aims to provide a platform for budding cricketers from the state as they rub shoulders with players who have represented Team India and various IPL franchises.
